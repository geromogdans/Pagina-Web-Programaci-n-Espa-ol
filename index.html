What is Private Neighborhood Management?

It’s an application developed to simplify the administration of private neighborhoods. You can register lots, link owners, track collections, and generate reports (Excel). It’s designed for small administrations that need simplicity and manageability.

User Guide

Our detailed user guide will walk you step-by-step through the software. You'll find information on how to register owners, manage payments, generate reports, and much more. The guide is designed to help you make the most of all the system’s features.

Detailed Features

Owner Management

Register each owner with complete data: name, contact, email, registration date, and associated lot. Each owner record allows you to add notes, view payment history, and attach documents. The system also validates duplicates and makes it easy to search by lot, name, or email for daily administrative tasks.

Payment and Debt Control

Manage monthly and extraordinary fees. The payment module records amount, date, and method, allowing partial payments. Generate lists of overdue fees, balances by owner, and consolidated statements by month and year. You can also record credit notes and adjustments, and export summaries for accounting.

Reports and Export

Generate financial and administrative reports in Excel: account statements, owner lists, expense summaries, and settlements. Reports can be filtered by date, block, lot, or owner. It also includes a full database export function for backups and audits.

Backup and Email Sending

Schedule periodic exports and automatically send backup files by email. Ideal for keeping off-site copies and facilitating audits. Files are generated in standard formats and can be manually downloaded anytime.

Security and Access Control

The system includes basic roles (administrator, operator) to restrict critical functions. It logs authorship of important changes and maintains traceability of payment and settlement operations, helping to meet internal control requirements.
